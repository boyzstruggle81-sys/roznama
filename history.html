Our Legacy

Decades of Truth. Unfiltered. Unbiased.

2015

The Genesis

The Foundation

Roznama Matti Ke Dewar was founded with a singular vision: to bring the truth to the common man. Starting from a small office, we published our very first edition with a pledge to never compromise on editorial integrity.

2022

Expanding Horizons

Regional Expansion

Recognizing the need for deep local news, we expanded our operations to include Bureau offices across Sindh. We recruited dedicated investigative reporters to cover stories that mainstream media often overlooked.

2025

Digital Frontier

Going Digital

We launched our ultra-fast online portal and high-definition E-paper. This modernization allowed our readers to access news from anywhere in the world, bringing our daily readership to historic heights.

NOW

The Voice of Truth

A Pillar of Journalism

Today, we stand as a symbol of trust. With a robust team of editors and correspondents, we continue our mission to serve the people with unbiased news, acting as the wall (Dewar) that protects the community's right to know.
